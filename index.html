Our story

build for all

Pioneer Academy opened its gates in 2026 in Sonapur, Kathalbari, a village in South Salmara Mankachar district, Assam. What started as a tin-roofed hall and a handful of wooden benches is now a full school community — teachers, students, and families gathering under one roof to learn together.

We teach in English, Assamese and Hindi, with a curriculum that runs up to Class X, and a staff room that believes discipline and warmth can sit side by side.
